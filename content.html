John Maeda seeks the potential in spaces where fields like computer science and graphic design or education and business mingle. His processes and projects tackle the future of design, technology and creative leadership. Like an exquisite corpse, this website designed for an ipad, lets you create a story that pieces together Maeda's upbringing, mentors, the interdisciplinary nature of his work, places he studied and worked in and his personality.


Interact with Exquisite Maeda by swiping right or left on any third of the screen. Click and hold on any third to read more.
